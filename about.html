About The Company

Nishant Consultancy

Nishant Consultancy is a research based management consulting firm providing decision making solutions to its customers worldwide, spanning a range of industries. Our core values are value, integrity and insight. Led by a team of dynamic industry experts, NC provides its customers with high value market research and advisory services that helps them identify new market opportunities, growth engines and innovative ways to capture the market share. As a result, our client leads rather than follow market trends. Not bound by legacy, NC cutting-edge research model leverages its decades of research knowledge and an increased use of technology as engines of innovation to deliver unique research value. Provided as an alternative to traditional market research, our reports do not just deliver data and knowledge rather highlights the insights in a more usable and interactive format for its clients. Nishant Consultancy is committed to assisting customers worldwide with their unique market research needs by providing them with comprehensive, value-added solutions and professional market intelligence services. NC employs the industry’s best-trained industry Analysts and Consultants, which advises and assists customers in meeting their full service market research and consulting requirements.
